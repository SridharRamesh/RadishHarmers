@antihydra1 What does it mean for log(B) to be the derivative of B^x at x = 0? It means that B^ε is about the same as B^0 + log(B)ε = 1 + log(B)ε for small ε.

Put another way, B^(1/N) is about the same as 1 + log(B)/N for large N.
