@jpdoane @stevenstrogatz For an arbitrary continuous function, any region with nonzero winding number contains a zero.

For holomorphic functions, we also have a converse: any region containing a zero has nonzero (and also non-negative) winding number. This is not true of arbitrary continuous functions.
