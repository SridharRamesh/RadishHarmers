@rahuldphys @marx_knopfler The LCM comment is equivalent to the Prime Number Theorem.

The LCM of the values from 1, 2, 3, ..., N is e^(ψ(N)), where ("the second Chebyshev function") ψ(N) is the sum of ("the von Mangoldt function") Λ(n) over n &lt; N, where Λ(power of prime p) = log(p) and is otherwise 0.
