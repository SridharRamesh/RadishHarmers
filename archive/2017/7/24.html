Timestamp: 2017-7-24 1:55:12

@sqrtnegative1 @sigfpe Now, let X be the self-referential proposition "If X is true, then P"; i.e., X = ☐X → P. By MP, ☐X &amp; ☐☐X →☐P. And ☐X → ☐☐X. So ☐X → ☐P.


Timestamp: 2017-7-24 1:59:10
