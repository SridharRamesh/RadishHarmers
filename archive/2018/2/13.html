@jiveass_baloney Suppose you have a 6,9-binary value X which agrees mod 2^n with a target value Y, and you want to extend it to agree mod 2^(n + 1) by adding either 6 * 2^n or 9 * 2^n. Well, precisely one such choice will work: 6 * 2^n if X and Y already agree mod 2^(n + 1) and 9 * 2^n otherwise.
